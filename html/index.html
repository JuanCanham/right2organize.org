Berkeley Tenant Protections and Right to Organize is a ballot measure that will update Berkeley's Tenant protection ordinance to:

  • Reduce the scope and abuse of exemptions to Berkeley's Tenant protection ordinance
  • Expand ability of the city to enforce it's rental registry program
  • Ensure tenants are aware of their rights during eviction proceedings
  • Protect the rights of tenants to form tenants associations and discuss issues with their landlords in good faith
  • Other amendments and improvements to the language in the ordinance that can be found here
